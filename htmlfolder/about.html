About Us

Mereinda Shack, one of today’s most renowned fast food restaurants, was established in 2019, as a quiet place where anyone could eat a burger or have a pizza that they loved. Our dedication to customers and quality food helps us to reach more today.

Our Story

2019

Idea

It all started from a single idea. We wanted to create a restaurant that would offer its customers their favorite fast food dishes - burgers, pizzas, and more.

2020

Opening

After making necessary preparations, completing our staff with experienced chefs and waiters, we were ready to meet new guests and visitors.

2022

Today

We are devoted to our customers and to buisness of our life - offering fast food dishes. And that’s why today we’re recognized as #1 Fast Food Chain in Philippines.
